About the event

A platform for changemakers shaping India's workforce future.

The Making India Employable Conference & Awards brings together leaders from education, industry, skilling, policy, and innovation to recognise measurable work in employability. MIECA celebrates initiatives that convert learning into livelihoods and ideas into national impact.
