About MIECA

Recognising impact where education meets employability.

MIECA brings together institutions, educators, employers, policymakers, and innovators who are improving the transition from learning to livelihoods.

Purpose

A conference and awards platform for measurable workforce outcomes.

The Making India Employable Conference & Awards celebrates individuals and institutions creating visible progress in skilling, higher education, workforce development, and industry-academia collaboration.

The platform recognises work that is practical, scalable, and aligned to India's evolving employment landscape. It is designed to surface models that others can learn from, support, and replicate.

Recognise

Celebrate real outcomes

Honouring work that improves employability, bridges skill gaps, and strengthens education-to-workforce pathways.

Convene

Bring leaders together

Connecting education, industry, policy, and innovation voices around the future of India's talent ecosystem.

Scale

Spotlight replicable models

Creating visibility for programmes and leaders with models that can inspire broader national impact.
