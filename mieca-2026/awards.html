Corporate Excellence Award

The Corporate Excellence Award recognises organisations that have made significant contributions to education and employability.

Companies may showcase work in areas such as:

  • Excellence in education or employability initiatives
  • Tech-driven learning solutions, inclusion, and diversity in the workplace
  • Innovative strategies for hiring and onboarding freshers
  • Employee engagement and retention programmes
  • Apprenticeship initiatives
  • CSR programmes focused on education or employability

Bharat Nirman Award

The Bharat Nirman Award honours institutions making a lasting contribution to higher education, employability, public service, and workforce development in India.

Nominee institutions should demonstrate excellence in areas such as:

  • Innovative educational programmes implemented at scale
  • Policy reforms in education and skills
  • Large-scale employability and skilling initiatives with measurable outcomes
  • Multi-stakeholder collaborations across industry, academia, and government
  • Sustainable impact in education, employability, and youth empowerment
